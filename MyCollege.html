About BMCC

Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is a public community college located in the heart of Manhattan, New York City. It is part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system. BMCC offers a wide range of academic programs designed to prepare students for career success or to transfer to four-year universities.

Academic Programs

  • Associate Degrees in Liberal Arts
  • Business and Technology Programs
  • Healthcare and Nursing Programs
  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) Programs
  • Arts and Humanities
